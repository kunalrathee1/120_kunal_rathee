Tribute to Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata

Industrialist • Philanthropist • Visionary Leader

About Him

Ratan Naval Tata is a prominent Indian industrialist and the former chairman of Tata Sons. Revered for his integrity, humility, and leadership, Ratan Tata transformed the Tata Group into a global enterprise. Under his leadership, the group acquired major companies like Jaguar Land Rover and Tetley. He’s also widely respected for his deep commitment to philanthropy through Tata Trusts.

Why We Admire Him

  • Former chairman of the Tata Group (1991–2012, interim 2016–17)
  • Architect of landmark acquisitions like JLR and Tetley
  • Launched the Tata Nano — the world’s most affordable car
  • Deeply committed to social impact through Tata Trusts
  • Awarded the Padma Bhushan and Padma Vibhushan
“I don’t believe in taking right decisions. I take decisions and then make them right.” – Ratan Tata
